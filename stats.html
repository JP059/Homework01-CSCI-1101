Pokemon Stats

Each pokemon has access to 6 different stats, including health, attack, defense, special attack, special defense, and speed. health and speed are self explanatory, health determines the amount of hitpoints a pokemon has, and the pokemon with the higher speed stat gets to go first in battle. The reason for the two different attack stats and two different defense stats is due to the fact that each attack move in the game is assigned as physical or special. Physical attacks' damage are determined by the attacking pokemon's attack stat, and the defending pokemon's defense stat, while moves assigned to the special category work the same way with the special attack and special defense stats.
